About the data

Data source(s)

  • Labour Force Survey, ONS

Time period

2014 to 2021

Geographic area

UK

What the data measures

The data shows the occupation level of 25 to 29 year olds in the UK between 2014 and 2021.

It also measures the percentage of 25 to 29 year olds in higher professional and lower working class occupations by region.

Regions are ranked in order and then divided into 5 groups, with each group having an equal number of regions.

Regions are based on where the young people who were surveyed lived when they were growing up.

Rates are rounded to 1 decimal place.

Things you need to know

The 5 social classes shown here are based on ONS’s socio-economic classification (‘NS-SEC’) as follows:

  • NS-SEC levels 1: higher professional
  • NS-SEC level 2: lower professional
  • NS-SEC levels 3 and 4: intermediate
  • NS-SEC levels 5 and 6: higher working class
  • NS-SEC levels 7 and 8: lower working class

Type of data

Survey data
