State of the Nation 2023: data about social mobility in the UK

State of the Nation is an annual report into social mobility in the UK. It uses original data and analysis from ​​the Social Mobility Commission (SMC).

State of the Nation uses the SMC’s own measurement framework, the Social Mobility Index. This looks at:

  • mobility outcomes – looking backwards
  • intermediate outcomes – looking at the present
  • drivers of social mobility – looking at the future

What you can do on this site

You can explore and download data by different breakdowns, such as region, sex and ethnicity.
